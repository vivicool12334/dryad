## Contractor Template
When adding a new contractor, use this format:
- **Name:** [name]
- **Services:** [invasive_removal | planting | soil_prep | mowing | site_assessment | tree_work]
- **Rate:** $[amount]/hr or $[amount]/job
- **Contact:** [email]
- **Wallet:** [Base address or "pending"]
- **Status:** [prospect | onboarding | active | inactive]
- **Added:** [date]
- **Jobs Completed:** [count]
- **Reliability Score:** [0-100]
- **Notes:** [any relevant context]

## Discovery Sources for Detroit Area
- Michigan EGLE licensed contractor database: https://www.michigan.gov/egle
- USDA NRCS contractor list: https://www.nrcs.usda.gov/contact/find-a-service-center
- Greening of Detroit volunteer network: https://www.greeningofdetroit.com/
- Michigan Conservation Stewards network (Nick's network)
- Detroit Conservation Corps
- Keep Growing Detroit: https://www.keepgrowingdetroit.org/
- Local arborists via ISA: https://www.treesaregood.org/findanarborist
- Saginaw Basin Land Conservancy (model for contractor partnerships)

## Onboarding Requirements
1. First job capped at $100 maximum
2. GPS-tagged before/after photos required via /Dryad/submit
3. Wallet address goes through 24-hour cooling-off period
4. Nick receives email notification for all new contractor onboarding
5. Reliability score starts at 50 (neutral) until 3+ jobs completed
