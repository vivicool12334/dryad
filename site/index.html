What it's worth

Every cared-for lot pays the neighbors back.

A blighted vacant lot drags adjacent home values down by 4 to 20 percent. The Penn-led randomized controlled trial on greened vacant lots showed the same parcels, once cleaned and planted, lift surrounding home values by 13 percent over six years and cut crime by 9 percent. Dryad is the autonomous version of that intervention, on parcels too small for any city program to bother with.
